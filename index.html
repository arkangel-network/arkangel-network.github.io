Arkangel Network

Welcome to Arkangel Network. We are a third-party labeling service for BlueSky. Our mission is to identify and label right-wing users using advanced scripting combined with human oversight to minimize false positives.

Our Labeling Criteria

We label individuals who are determined by our moderators to be right of center. This includes movements such as the trans-exclusionary radical feminist (TERF) movement and members of the "red/brown" alliance who may identify as left-wing but promote far-right ideologies.

These groups advocate positions that align with the objectives of the global far right. Therefore, they are included in our labeling regardless of their personal ideological identifications.

Removal Process

For False Positives: If you believe you have been labeled incorrectly, please subscribe to the labeler and report your account. Select "Other" and include "Appeal" along with the reasons why you believe it is a false positive.

For Ideological Changes: To be removed due to a genuine change in ideology, we require evidence of a disavowal of past behaviors and a demonstrable change in conduct. If you feel you have evolved in your views, please report your account for a review.
